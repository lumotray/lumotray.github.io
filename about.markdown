# About LumoTray

LumoTray is a free, lightweight Windows 11 system tray app that gives you more control over your desktop without the bloat.

## Why does it exist?

Windows 11's built-in tray options are pretty limited. You get a context menu and that's about it. I kept thinking there had to be a better way — a way to customize the tray, set wallpapers per-monitor, add hot corners, and manage your lockscreen — without installing a suite of bloated utilities or writing custom code.

So I built it myself.

## The philosophy

- **Free and functional.** The app works fully out of the box. No watermarks, no trial period, no features locked behind paywalls.
- **Lightweight.** Built to use minimal resources. It sits in your tray, does its job, and gets out of the way.
- **No subscriptions.** The $19 license is a one-time payment to remove the weekly nag and add your brand name. That's it.
- **Solo project.** LumoTray is built and maintained by one person (me, Ved). Feedback and ideas are welcome.

## What's included?

LumoTray comes with five built-in modules:

1. **Custom Menu** — Personal launcher for your most-used apps and tools
2. **Wallpaper Manager** — Multi-monitor wallpaper support
3. **Lockscreen Manager** — Customize your pre-login screen
4. **Screensaver Modes** — Classic and creative screensaver options
5. **Hot Corners** — Trigger actions from screen corners
